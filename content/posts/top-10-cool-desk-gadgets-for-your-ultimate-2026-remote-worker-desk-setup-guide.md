## Technology to Boost Productivity

### External Monitor

Adding an external monitor to your remote worker desk setup can significantly increase your workspace and productivity. With more screen real estate, you can have multiple applications open at once, making it easier to multitask. However, this requires additional desk space, so plan accordingly. Consider a monitor with adjustable brightness and color settings to ensure optimal viewing conditions.

### Noise-Canceling Headphones

Noise-canceling headphones are invaluable for remote workers who need to block out distractions and maintain focus. These headphones can help you concentrate on your work, especially in noisy environments. However, they can also isolate you from your surroundings, so use them judiciously. Look for headphones with adjustable noise cancellation settings to balance your need for focus and awareness of your environment.

## Entertainment and Motivation

### Desk Plants

Desk plants can improve air quality and enhance your mental health, making your workspace more enjoyable. However, they require maintenance, including watering and occasional pruning. ### Desk Speaker

A desk speaker can provide background music or podcasts, adding a touch of entertainment to your workday. While it can be a distraction, a speaker can also help you stay motivated and energized. Choose a speaker with good sound quality and a compact design to fit seamlessly into your desk setup.

When choosing gadgets for your remote worker desk setup, it's important to consider not only functionality but also how they integrate into your existing workspace. For instance, if you're working in a smaller space, look for multi-functional gadgets that can serve multiple purposes.
